Privacy Policy and Terms of Service for Suture

Date: June 27, 2026

Thank you for using Suture ("the App"). This document outlines our commitment to your privacy and the terms you agree to when using the App.

Privacy Policy

  1. Account Registration & Personal Information. To access certain features of the App—such as syncing system materials and participating in the 1v1 Quiz Arena—you must register for an account. When you sign up, we collect and store your email address and authentication credentials. This information is used solely to authenticate your identity, manage your account, and host your online gameplay profile. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.
  2. Local vs. Cloud Data Storage. Suture is built on a hybrid storage model. Any custom questions (MCQs), notes, or tags you create are stored only locally on your device. We do not have access to your custom content, and it is never sent to our servers. If you sign in, the App communicates with our servers to download administrative quiz questions (system materials) and synchronize real-time multiplayer states.
  3. Real-Time Multiplayer Data (1v1 Quiz Arena). When you use the 1v1 Quiz Arena feature, the App transmits live gameplay inputs, including your speed, score updates, and answers, to facilitate real-time matches with your opponent. We store match records (such as game lobby IDs, final scores, and the winner's user ID) on our servers to maintain matchmaking integrity and user stats.
  4. Third-Party Services. We use Supabase as our backend-as-a-service provider to handle secure authentication, database storage, and real-time matchmaking communication. Your credentials and online gameplay statistics are stored securely on Supabase servers in compliance with their privacy and security protocols.
  5. No Ads or Trackers. The App is free of advertisements and third-party advertising trackers. We do not track your activity outside of the App.
  6. Educational Content Disclaimer. The App provides previous year questions and notes for educational purposes only. It is not intended to provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Users should consult qualified medical professionals for any health-related decisions.
